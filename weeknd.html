The

Weeknd

The Weeknd, whose real name is Abel Tesfaye, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and producer known for his unique blend of R&B, pop, and electronic music. Rising to fame with his mixtapes in 2011, he has since become a global sensation with hits like "Blinding Lights," "Can't Feel My Face," and "The Hills." His music often explores themes of love, heartbreak, and self-discovery, paired with innovative production.
